    Who I am

    Who am I beyond labels?

    I am Arkan Mahmoudi, a person whose life has been shaped by curiosity, persistence and the desire to understand people more deeply.

    I studied psychology because understanding human beings was never just an academic subject for me. I have always been interested in emotions, mental struggles, relationships, personality and the invisible things that influence human life. Psychology for me is not only a field of study — it is also a way of seeing people with more patience and understanding.

    I currently work in educational counseling and I care about helping people, especially in educational and personal contexts. I believe that guidance is not about giving ready-made answers; sometimes it is simply helping people see themselves more clearly.

    I do not see myself as a collection of titles. I am simply someone trying to build a meaningful life while continuing to learn, understand and improve.

    My journey

    Early

    A curious beginning

    Grew up with a deep curiosity about people — why they feel what they feel, why they do what they do. This curiosity became the compass for everything that followed.

    University

    Studying psychology

    Chose psychology not as a career strategy but as a genuine need to understand human experience more deeply. The study of mind and behavior felt like learning a language I had always half-spoken.

    Now

    Educational counseling

    Working as an educational counselor — sitting with people in their uncertainty, helping them see their own path a little more clearly. This work taught me that listening is a skill most people underestimate.

    Ongoing

    Learning technology

    Entered the world of web development with curiosity. HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and now further. I enjoy the process of building things — taking an idea and turning it into something real and visible.
